# Black Wind King in Black Myth: Wukong

The Black Wind King is a powerful Yaoguai King located in the Black Wind Cave on Black Wind Mountain. He claims to be the ruler of Black Wind Mountain, showcasing formidable strength and a fierce, skillful fighting style.
Once an ordinary demon, the Black Wind King fell into greed and the desire for power, ultimately transforming into the demon king of Black Wind Mountain. His story reflects themes of desire and downfall, symbolizing the loss of self in the pursuit of power.

## Strategy

The Black Wind King's combat style is characterized by high burst damage and agile attacks. Players must remain vigilant during the fight, using the environment to dodge and counterattack. Observing his attack patterns will help players find opportunities to strike back while staying alert to the flames and storms he summons to avoid damage.

## Moves

### Smash Attack

The Black Wind King slams the ground with great force, causing area damage. Players should dodge promptly to avoid being hit.

<img src="https://image.informedainews.com/bwk1.gif" alt="Black Myth: Wukong"/>

### Flame Fist

He delivers a powerful left punch, then lunges forward with his right claw, attempting to grab and consume the enemy. This move has a long recovery time, allowing players to deal damage in response.

<img src="https://image.informedainews.com/bwk2.gif" alt="Black Myth: Wukong"/>

### Ground Fire Eruption

The Black Wind King strikes the ground, causing flames to erupt from beneath. Players must quickly evade to avoid being burned.

<img src="https://image.informedainews.com/bwk3.gif" alt="Black Myth: Wukong"/>

### Black Wind Charge

He transforms into a dark wind, rapidly moving and charging at the player, dealing substantial damage. Players should pay attention to the wind's movement and dodge accordingly.
