# Stats

The Stats in 'Black Myth: Wukong' include basic attributes and the Four Calamities Resistances, which are as follows:

## Basic Attributes

- Health
- Mana
- Qi Power
- Attack
- Defense
- Qi Recovery
- Critical Hit Rate
- Critical Damage
- Damage Increase
- Damage Reduction

## Four Calamities Resistances

- Cold Resistance
- Fire Resistance
- Poison Resistance
- Lightning Resistance
