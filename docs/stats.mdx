# Stats

Attributes include Life, Mana, and Stamina, along with Basic Attributes and Four Calamity Resistances.

Basic Attributes: Attack, Defense, Critical Hit Rate, Critical Hit Multiplier, Damage Boost, and Damage Reduction.

Four Calamity Resistances: Cold Resistance, Fire Resistance, Poison Resistance, and Lightning Resistance.
